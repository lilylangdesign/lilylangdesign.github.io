Sisters Road to Freedom, Inc. is a nonprofit in Lexington, KY. They assist in providing clothes, organic food, workshops, seminars,etc. This was a rebrand project completed for the Design It Forward KY 2023 competition. This was completed with two other designers in six hours. The goal of this project was to rebrand this non-profit organization and create collateral to go with the new brand.
