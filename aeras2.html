Aéras Skate Co. is a skateboard company concept project. Inspired by its meaning in Greek, “air”, Aéras Skate Co. allows skaters to embrace their passion for skateboarding while gliding through the world around them. The goal of this project was to create a skateboard company brand as well as the design for one skateboard.
