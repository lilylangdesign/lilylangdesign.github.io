Thrive Hygienics is a subscription box company concept project that focuses on providing necessary hygiene products to teenagers and young adults. This personalized hygiene kit has many options for products. Some of the product options consist of deodorant, shampoo, conditioner, body wash, face wash, toothbrush, and toothpaste. The goal of this project was to create a subscription box company with a physical mock up of products and a box for one variation of the options the company provides.
